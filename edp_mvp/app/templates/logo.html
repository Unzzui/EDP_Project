Usage Guidelines

Command Center Usage
Terminal-inspired logo with geometric clipped corners and pulsing core. Use for operational dashboards, monitoring systems, and technical interfaces.
Color Requirements
Primary: #00ff88 (high-visibility green)
Secondary: #ff0066 (alert pink)
Background: #000000 (pure black)
Typography
Space Grotesk 800 weight, uppercase, 0.15em letter-spacing. "ADMIN" subtitle in JetBrains Mono 500 weight.
Executive Suite Usage
Professional diamond logo with gradient background and subtle highlight. Use for boardroom presentations, corporate communications, and executive interfaces.
Color Requirements
Primary: #0066cc (professional blue)
Gradient: #0066cc to #1d4ed8
Background: #fafafa (warm off-white)
Typography
Inter 700 weight, -0.02em letter-spacing. "Admin" subtitle in Inter 500 weight, proper case.
